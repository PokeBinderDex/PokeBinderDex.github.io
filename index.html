How To Use Your PDF Collections

Purchase on PayHip

Select your preferred collection and complete your purchase on our PayHip store. You'll receive a downloadable PDF file immediately after payment.

Printing Recommendations

Print only the pages you want on A4 paper. For U.S. users or non-A4 formats: Don't resize the document - instead, select "crop margins" or similar option to preserve the correct card dimensions.

Cut and Collect

Carefully cut out your cards along the guidelines. Consider using cardstock for a sturdier finish, or laminate your cards for long-lasting durability.

Enjoy Your Collection

Organize your cards in a binder and enjoy your custom Pokémon collection!
